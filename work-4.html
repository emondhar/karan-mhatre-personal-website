Practo India hospital listings

Launched hospital listings on Practo.com, listed +6,000 hospitals and acquired new users

Opportunity

To improve user acquisition we were expanding the practo.com offering to include hospital listings and attract a new segment of users.

Practo had figured out doctor listings, but new understanding was needed to venture into hospital listings.

Solution

We approached the problem from a first principals mindset and started conducting ground level research.

We spoke to hospital staff, and understood consumer behaviour in this space, to finalise what data parameters we needed to collect.

We completed a 6 month on ground operations program to collect hospital data.

We built a new product for listing hospitals.

We improved SEO for our top hospital pages to improve user acquisition.

Through our customer research we realised that the hospital search journey begins from a need for a medical treatment. This insight lead us to build a Procedure Search product that educated a patient in understanding how to select a hospital for each procedure and provided necessary additional data points to improve their decision making.

Where I added value

Carried out research to thoroughly understand the patient to hospital journey.

Identified oppurtunities for improving SEO (for example, with creation of pages like “Top 10 cardiology hospitals in Mumbai”)

Personally carried out data collection from hospitals to understand the feasibility of collection.

Built and maintained relationships with hospitals to ease in the data collection.

Setup success metrics for the product, and drafted a product roadmap.

Managed feature requests from sales and marketing teams and balanced those with consumer needs to create and maintain the product roadmap.

Impact

Listed 6K of the top hospitals in India.

Opened up a new revenue stream in terms of hospital ads.

Improved customer acquisition through search engines
