Lauched a rewards program for a mass media marketing campaign with GOQii that acquired a large set of new customers

Opportunity

GOQii wanted to carry out a mass marketing campaign to drive customer acquisition.

We needed a hook and added levels of motivation for new customers to want to try GOQii.

An impediment in GOQii’s vision of a Healthy India, was the problem of health myths being circulated on social media.

Solution

Since HQ Trivia had made a big impact in terms of customer app engagement, we decided to build a live quizzing platform around health.

We designed a solution for long term engagement using extrinsic rewards on healthy habits.

We designed a referral program to help accelarate customer acquisition.

Where I added value

Wireframing (Hands on with Sketch)

Goal setting - Defining and monitoring success metrics to drive further product decisions.

Usability testing for the new gamification system to determine the right difficulty levels.

Game designing in terms of points, levels, and rewards.

Coordination with marketing teams to align (in terms of voice and branding) with the larger mass media campaign.

Designing the notification strategy to maximize engagement through the campaign.

Impact

Acquired a large number of customers.

Improved Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by providing a complimenting referral program to the marketing campaign efforts.

Improved the average time spent per day per user on the app significantly.
