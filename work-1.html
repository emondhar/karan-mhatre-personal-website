Launched a zero to one live streaming platform for GOQii and grew it to a significant MAU base.

Opportunity

Video consumption is a rising trend online and we wanted to leverage this to provide better health services to our customers.

We had customers ask coaches to recommend workouts.

Customers were getting accustomed to consuming information over video rather than long form text.

Solution

There already existed OTT platforms for health. Users could find workout classes on Youtube as well.

But we realised that in health, there isn’t a one size fits all solution. We needed to bring in personalisation.

Our solution was to build a live video streaming health classes platform that allowed curated coaches to host live video classes in which they could engage and interact with our users.

We have now expanded that service to include courses (a set of classes). This has helped us improve retention on the platform.
